Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lovely Img Tools ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our website.

1. Image processing

All image editing tools on this website (Compress, Image to PDF, Crop, Passport Photo Creator) run entirely in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. Your photos, signatures, and documents are never uploaded to our servers and are never stored, viewed, or shared by us. Once you close or refresh the page, the image data is cleared from your browser's memory.

2. Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage only to remember your light/dark theme preference. We do not use tracking cookies of our own.

3. Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

4. Analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand overall traffic patterns, such as number of visitors and popular pages. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

5. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect or store your uploaded images, photos, or signatures.
  • We do not require account creation, login, or personal information to use any tool.
  • We do not sell any data to third parties.

6. Children's privacy

Our tools are intended for general use by students and the public. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
