About Lovely Img Tools

Lovely Img Tools was built for one simple reason: filling government exam forms should not be harder than the exam itself. Every year, thousands of students filling SSC, Railway, Banking, UPSC, Police, CUET and university admission forms get stuck at the photo upload step — wrong file size, wrong dimensions, or wrong format.

What we offer

We built four focused tools that solve this problem in a few clicks: an Image Compressor to fit strict file size limits, an Image to PDF converter for document uploads, a Crop tool for exact pixel/cm/mm/inch dimensions, and a Passport Photo Creator for forms that need a name and date of birth printed below the photo.

Your privacy comes first

Every tool on this website runs entirely inside your browser. Your photos are processed on your own device using JavaScript — they are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never seen by anyone. Once you close the tab, your image is gone.

Who we built this for

Students preparing for SSC, Railway (RRB), IBPS/SBI Banking exams, UPSC, State PSC exams, Police recruitment, CUET, and university admissions — anyone who needs a quick, reliable way to get their photo form-ready without installing an app or visiting a photo studio.

Free, always

Lovely Img Tools is, and will remain, free to use. We may show ads to keep the website running, but no tool will ever be locked behind a paywall or signup.
