Photo tools built for Indian government exam forms

Lovely Img Tools is a free online toolkit made for students filling SSC, Railway (RRB), IBPS/SBI Banking, UPSC, Police recruitment, CUET and university admission forms. Most of these forms reject applications for wrong photo size, oversized signatures, or wrong file format — and fixing this on a phone is usually confusing. This toolkit solves that in four simple steps: upload, edit, preview, and download.

Every tool runs directly in your browser. Your photo is never uploaded to a server, which means it is faster and more private than most online photo editors.

Frequently asked questions

Is Lovely Img Tools free to use?

Yes, all four tools — Compressor, Image to PDF, Crop, and Passport Photo Creator — are completely free with no sign-up required.

Is my photo uploaded to a server?

No. Every tool processes your image directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never leaves your device.

Which exams can I use this for?

SSC, Railway, IBPS/SBI Banking, UPSC, Police recruitment, CUET, and most university admission forms that require a specific photo size, signature size, or PDF upload.
