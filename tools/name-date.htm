Why add name & date to a photo

Some exam forms, ID cards, and attendance documents require a photo with the applicant's name and date printed directly on it. This tool lets you type your name and date, choose position, font, size and color, and download the finished photo in seconds — no design software needed.

Frequently asked questions

Can I place the text anywhere on the photo?

Yes — choose from top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right, or a full-width bottom bar.

Will the text be readable on a busy background?

Turn on "Text background" to add a semi-transparent strip behind the text for better readability.
