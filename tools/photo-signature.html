Why combine photo and signature

Some exam forms have a single upload field that requires your photo and signature joined into one image, either side-by-side or stacked, within a strict file size limit. This tool lets you upload both separately, choose a layout, and download one combined image ready to upload.

Frequently asked questions

Can I control the size of the final image?

Yes, set the combined width and height in pixels before downloading.

What if my photo and signature have different backgrounds?

Both images are placed on a plain white canvas by default, so any background mismatch is minimised.
